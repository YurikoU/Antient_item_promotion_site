ABOUT

A chakram is handy. You can thread the center of a chakram through your arm and carry it while not using it.

Over a battle, using a chakram is a great choice. You can even slice off arms and legs with ease.

Each chakram is light and usually weigh only about 150-500 gram per one. You can bring several chakrams at once.

Looks more stylish than other common weapons such as a knife or a bow. It is usually decorated with fine gold decoration, florals using the koftghari technique.
