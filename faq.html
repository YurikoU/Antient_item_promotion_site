FAQ

Q: Can I throw and catch a chakram like a frisbee?
A: No. Unlike a frisbee, you cannot grab a chakram in the air as the outer edge is extremely sharpened.
Q: Who had used a chakram mainly?
A: A chakram had been common among the high ranking Indian Sikhs in Northern India.
Q: How long ago had been a chakram used?
A: A chakram had been used from the 16th to 19th centuries commonly.
Q: Is there another way to throw a chakram except horizontally?
A: Yes. A chakram had also been thrown vertically often.
Q: Is it possible to carry multiple chakrams at once?
A: Yes. Chakram users had often worn several chakrams, in the maximum eight, since a chakram weighs only 150-500 grams per each, and it is possible to wear them around their necks or on their turbans.
